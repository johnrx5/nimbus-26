# NovaMed Provider Personas (from Notion - Product Philosophy)

## Core Problem
"Your patients are asking for GLP-1s, peptides, and hormone therapy. If you can't offer it, they'll find someone who can." — Usually a faceless telehealth company.

## Two Paths, One Platform
- **WITH Prescribers:** Doctors review intakes, prescribe directly. EMR + Payments + Pharmacy + Telehealth integrated.
- **WITHOUT Prescribers:** Chiropractors, med spas, wellness centers — leverage our prescriber network.

## The Customer: The Overloaded Provider
Common thread: Clinicians drowning in admin burden while trying to practice medicine.

### Hierarchy of Needs
1. Financial stability (cash flow, not chasing AR)
2. Workflow reliability (things just work)
3. Admin burden reduction (fewer decisions, fewer clicks)
4. Clinical confidence (prescribing safely)
5. Revenue growth (downstream of all four)

### What They're Actually Buying
"They are not pursuing practice transformation. They are preventing operational collapse."

## The Four Personas

### 1. Dr. Marcus — The Business Scaler (Revenue Engine)
- **Profile:** 3+ clinics, multi-site operator
- **Track:** Professional/Premier
- **Needs:** Efficiency, revenue per patient, brand consistency
- **Why Nimbus:** Eliminate AR, white-label, standardize protocols, immediate revenue
- **Mindset:** Business-first, efficiency-obsessed, scale-focused
- **Fears:** Operational inconsistency, margin erosion
- **Design:** ROI-first messaging, multi-clinic dashboards, low friction onboarding

### 2. Dr. Sofia — The Med Spa/Aesthetic Owner (Volume Driver)
- **Profile:** High-volume, cash-pay aesthetic/wellness
- **Track:** Starter/Professional
- **Needs:** Enter peptide/GLP-1/hormone space safely
- **Why Nimbus:** Safety guardrails (IntelliHealth), clinical guidance, standalone portal
- **Mindset:** Ambitious but cautious
- **Fears:** Clinical mistakes, steep learning curves
- **Design:** Safety-first messaging, pre-built questionnaires, "Clinical Co-Pilot"

### 3. Dr. James — The Burnt-Out/New Physician (Retention Engine)
- **Profile:** Solo practitioner transitioning from insurance-based care
- **Track:** Starter → Professional
- **Needs:** Focus on patients not billing, cash-pay revenue streams
- **Why Nimbus:** AI Co-Pilot, streamlined intake, zero cost entry, education
- **Mindset:** Cautious, overwhelmed, wants simplicity
- **Fears:** Looking incompetent, failed experiments
- **Design:** Gentle UX, demo-driven onboarding, zero upfront cost, starter tier

### 4. Dr. Kim — The Non-Prescribing Clinician (Expansion Frontier)
- **Profile:** PT, acupuncturist, nutritionist, health coach
- **Track:** Starter → Prescribing Network
- **Needs:** Prescribing capability through licensed network
- **Why Nimbus:** Prescribe under own brand, keep revenue in-house, white-label
- **Mindset:** Entrepreneurial, relationship-focused, scope-limited
- **Fears:** Legal/compliance exposure
- **Design:** "Prescribing without a license" positioning, revenue calculator, turnkey

## Three Core Principles
1. **Operational Relief Over Practice Transformation** — not becoming different, running better
2. **Readiness Determines Journey, Not Practice Size** — meet where they are
3. **Language That Empowers, Never Overwhelms** — never make them feel judged
